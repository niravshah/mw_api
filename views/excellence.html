Attract and Retain valuable Employees.

Skills, People, Teams, Motivation, Leadership and Performance are the key themes on every business leaders objectives for 2016. These themes will become even more important than ever if we consider the rather gloomy forecasts of experts, who warn of the onset of a profound human resources crisis in the coming years. A company's competitive advantage in such times will definitely be people who will drive excellence in services and products to customers.

We start from the premise that no one is perfect, but a team can achieve perfection. Moreover, a simple punctual action is not sufficient for the development of an employee or a team. A simple training skills development must be backed by a number of activities like team building and experiential learning aimed at concrete and consistent progress over time and monitor the results.

Further for any company working in a highly competitive environment, employee attachment to brand is becoming extremely essential. Such attachment can be developed not through only attractive salaries but by helping employees focus on the company's core values.

In this context, we have created a comprehensive employee development, called - "Team Excellence - 7 steps from individual to team success" meant to produce results and developments to operational or functional teams.

Team Excellence program combines the benefits of training programs, team building and social responsibility, bringing them together under the umbrella of a program of experiential learning, easily adaptable to each company.

How can Experential Learning help your Organization?

Our program helps develop the following key attributes in your employees

  • Self Confidence - helping the participant to differentiate between vanity and confidence itself
  • Internal Motivation - helping the participant to regain inner strength to act and perform on a regular basis
  • Competitiveness - helping the participant to control their anxiety related to competition and to perform well under pressure
  • Perseverance - equipping the participant with the tools (psychological and professional) needed to accomplish the task
  • Concentration - participant learning how to trigger and keep their focus the goal during the sales process
  • Psycho-Emotional Balance - providing the necessary scientific methods to help participants surpass the limits of their virtual experience, psychological mind and keep the balance on core performance
  • Strategic thinking - engaging participant to act according to plans and strategies set in advance.
