Employee Retention

Of the 10.5k+ recent Job changers interviewed by LinkedIn, 49% stated lack of career advancement opportunities as the top reason to leave an organization. A further 59% stated better career path/opportunities as the key reason to join a new organization.47% of the respondent said that their new role was a better fit for their skills. And 42% felt they would have better opportunity to make an impact.

Employee Engagement

For any company working in a highly competitive environment, employee attachment to brand is becoming extremely essential. Such attachment can be developed not through only attractive salaries but by helping employees focus on the company's core values and developing their professional skills and strengths through experiential learning programs.

Key Features of MoveWithin

Private

Only employees within your organization can access your listing page

Workflow

Everything in one place. From authoring new listing to reviewing, editing and going live.

Integrated

MoveWithin can integrate with your organizations intranet. Your users don't need to create separate accounts.

KPIs

Our extremely strong analytics module gives you, for the first time, a true indication of your attrition and retention rates

Talent Brand

MoveWithin is not just a job listing page. We offer you a platform to create powerful talent brand pages that can be shared with talent internally

Customizable

MoveWithin is built ground up to be customizable to fit within your existing HR process with a low cost of implementation.
